About CineList

Born from a passion for the silver screen, CineList is more than just a tracking tool—it’s a digital sanctuary for those who live and breathe cinema. We believe that every film you watch is a chapter in your personal history, a memory waiting to be preserved in an era of endless streaming and fleeting digital noise.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between watching and remembering. In a world where it's easier than ever to consume content, it’s also easier than ever to forget what truly moved us. CineList provides a dedicated space where your cinematic journey is celebrated, allowing you to curate your own archive, refine your taste, and share your unique perspective with a global community of enthusiasts.

  • Personalized Archives: Keep a detailed, beautiful log of every film you’ve seen, complete with dates, personal ratings, and in-depth reviews.
  • Community & Connection: Beyond the data, we are a social network. Discover hidden gems through the eyes of others, explore community-curated collections, and connect with fans who share your specific obsession—from 17th-century folk horror to modern sci-fi epics.
  • Curated Discovery: Our platform is designed to help you cut through the clutter. We offer tools to help you organize your watchlist and find exactly what to watch next based on authentic human experiences, not just algorithms.
"We don't just watch movies; we experience them. CineList is here to make sure those experiences are never forgotten."
